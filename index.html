Support Center

Asset store verification hub & frequently asked questions.

Our standard commercial license allows you to use the audio assets in unlimited commercial projects, including video games, films, YouTube videos, and podcasts. You may not resell, redistribute, or re-package the raw audio files as standalone assets.

All our asset packs are delivered in industry-standard 24-bit / 96kHz or 44.1kHz WAV format for maximum fidelity. We also provide optimized OGG formats where applicable for direct game engine integration (Unity, Unreal Engine, Godot).

Due to the digital nature of our products, we generally do not offer refunds once the files have been downloaded. However, if you experience technical issues with the files or accidental duplicate purchases, please contact our support team to resolve the matter.
